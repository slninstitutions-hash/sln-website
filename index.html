About SLN Institutions

SLN Institutions -- Sri Lakshmi Narasimha E.M High School in Karvetinagar focuses on academic excellence, discipline, and value-based education. We offer education from Pre-Primary through Intermediate, led by experienced faculty and modern facilities.

Vision

To create a learning community where every student develops academic excellence, strong values, creativity, and life skills to succeed.

Mission

  • High-quality education with discipline
  • Develop leadership and problem-solving
  • Focus on academics, sports and arts

Academics & Faculty

We follow the Andhra Pradesh State Board curriculum in English medium with a strong focus on fundamentals and continuous assessment.

Classes Offered

Pre-Primary to 10th Class & Intermediate (MPC / BiPC / CEC)

Subjects

English, Telugu, Maths, Science, Social, Computers, and more.

Faculty

Qualified teachers, regular training, student-centred teaching methods, special instructors for labs and sports.
